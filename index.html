Fragments of Insomnia: Original Soundtrack for Another March Through Reverie

  • A story-driven sci-fi game inspired by Metroid.
  • The soundtrack, composed of 18 ambiotic scores, captures the energy in each of the game's scenes.
  • Dreamy synths and mellow keys ease the tension during calm moments, while percussion and bass intensify fast-paced encounters with bosses.
  • Additionally, as the player moves faster, the drums get louder.

    • Check out my favorite track: 16 - Earth's Revival
