Fragments of Insomnia: Original Soundtrack for Another March Through Reverie

In the distant future, humans discover that they are the most technologically advanced species in the universe. After establishing the most powerful intergalactic army, the human race conquers much of the universe and exploits the other races in pursuit of infinite power. After a millennia of bloody resistance against the dominating human race, the allied species of the universe have successfully driven the humans back to their home world, Earth. Merely 11 humans remain on Earth, but only one creature dares to challenge the dying race on their own turf.

  • A story-driven sci-fi game inspired by Metroid.
  • The soundtrack, composed of 18 ambient, electrical scores, captures the energy in each of the game's scenes.
  • Dreamy synths and mellow keys ease the tension during calm moments, while percussion and bass intensify fast-paced encounters with bosses.
  • Additionally, as the player moves faster, the drums get louder.
